Taylor

A contemporary wedge-serif typeface inspired by the chunky newsprint faces of the turn of the century

Taylor is characterized by it's large, triangular serifs, wide aperture, and subtly angular curves. While the Black weight lends itself well to bold, impactful headlines, the mid-weights work well for body copy, with high legibility down to 10pt. The full feature set of the family includes standard and discretionary ligatures, proportional and tabular numerals, fractions and mathematical symbols, standard and extended punctuation, diacritical marks and language support for 50+ latin languages.
